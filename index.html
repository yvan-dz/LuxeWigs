Frequently Asked Questions

How do I care for my wig?

We recommend washing your wig with gentle products, letting it air dry, and styling it gently to extend its lifespan.

What are the shipping times?

Orders are typically shipped within 2-3 business days. Delivery time depends on your location, but usually takes between 5 to 10 days.

Can I return a wig if it doesn't suit me?

Yes, you can return an unworn wig within 14 days of receipt for an exchange or refund.
